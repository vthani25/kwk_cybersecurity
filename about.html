ABOUT

Purpose

This website, CyberEd, was built to make cybersecurity education engaging and accessible. It helps students and everyday users understand real-time cyber threats and learn practical ways to stay safe online.

What the Project Does

We have visualized global cyber threat data and shows how attacks such as phishing, malware, and data breaches occur. Through interactive dashboards and visuals, users can explore how these threats work and discover how to prevent them.

Background & Inspiration

CyberEd was developed during the Kode With Klossy Summer Program, inspired by a passion for technology and a desire to raise cybersecurity awareness. This project combines education and visualization to show how informed users can make the internet a safer place.

Why It Matters

With millions of cyberattacks happening every day, understanding how and why data is compromised has never been more important. CyberEd encourages users to learn about cyberthreats visually and discover the implications of their choices online.

About the Creator

Created by Thanishkka Vijayabaskar. I'm a student passionate about technology, cybersecurity, and empowering others to use technology safely. This project grew from real-world security concerns and a vision of building a more secure digital world.
