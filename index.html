About the Project

This project takes complex cybersecurity data and displays it as a clear, interactive story. Users can explore data on different vulnerability types, such as social engineering, unpatched software, weak passwords, and zero-day exploits, and understand how each contributes to global cyber risk. It also highlights the financial and human toll of cyberattacks through a world map.

Use the buttons and sections to dive deeper into the data!
